Drone Airspace Safety Advisor
Agent: When user picks a flight location and time, the agent checks regulated airspace zones, fetches active flight notices and restrictions, pulls current wind and visibility data, computes the safe altitude ceiling, and generates a step-by-step permit checklist if the zone is restricted.
Frontend: Map-based Location Picker — clicking a location and entering a time triggers the agent | Airspace Overlay Map — agent-coloured zones: green (free), yellow (conditional), red (restricted) | NOTAM List Panel — agent-fetched active notices near the selected location | Weather Safety Gauges — wind speed and visibility readings fetched by the agent with safe/unsafe indicator | Permit Guide Page — agent-generated step-by-step checklist for restricted zones
