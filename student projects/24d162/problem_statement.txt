Smart Recipe & Grocery Planner
Agent: When user submits dietary preferences and number of meals, the agent fetches matching recipes, calculates total nutrition across the week, checks ingredient prices from supermarket data, builds an optimised shopping list, and schedules meals on a calendar.
Frontend: Preference Setup Page — dietary filters, servings, and budget inputs; "Build My Week" button triggers the agent | Weekly Meal Calendar — drag-and-drop cards generated by the agent, each showing recipe image and macros | Nutrition Dashboard — agent-calculated macro breakdown with pie charts per day | Shopping List Page — agent-grouped by store section with total cost estimate and checkboxes
