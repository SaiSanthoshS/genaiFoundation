Voter Awareness & Representative Tracker
Agent: When user enters their constituency, the agent fetches the elected representative's profile, retrieves their recent parliamentary voting record, analyses their stance on key policy areas, compares those stances to the user's stated priorities, and suggests concrete civic actions based on any misalignment.
Frontend: Constituency Lookup Page — address input; submission triggers the agent | Representative Profile Page — agent-assembled bio, party, and tenure summary | Voting Record Table — agent-fetched bills with vote, date, and agent-assigned stance tag | Priority Match Score Chart — agent-computed alignment between representative's votes and user's stated priorities | Civic Action Guide — agent-generated list of actions (contact rep, sign petition, attend town hall)
