Sports Injury Risk & Lineup Predictor
Agent: When user selects a team and upcoming fixture, the agent fetches the current injury report, retrieves three seasons of player performance data, runs a model to predict each player's match availability, estimates the probable lineup, and generates fantasy sports scoring projections.
Frontend: Team & Fixture Selector Page — team dropdown and match date; "Predict Lineup" triggers the agent | Roster Grid — agent-assessed player cards showing form, injury status, and predicted availability percentage | Predicted Lineup Visualisation — agent-placed players on a pitch or field graphic | Injury Timeline Chart — agent-tracked injury history per player with return dates | Fantasy Score Projections Table — agent-estimated points per player for the upcoming match
