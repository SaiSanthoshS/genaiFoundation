Pet Adoption Matcher
Agent: When user submits their family profile, the agent queries adoption databases for currently available pets, retrieves breed temperament and care-requirement data, scores compatibility between each animal and the family's lifestyle, filters by distance to the shelter, and pre-fills an adoption enquiry form for the top match.
Frontend: Family Profile Setup — home type, activity level, experience, and allergy inputs; "Find My Pet" triggers the agent | Pet Match Cards — agent-scored animals with photo, name, compatibility percentage, and shelter distance | Pet Detail Page — agent-fetched breed info, temperament, health notes, and shelter contact | Compatibility Radar Chart — agent-generated profile showing energy, grooming, and space needs | Adoption Enquiry Form — agent pre-filled with family and pet details; user reviews and submits
