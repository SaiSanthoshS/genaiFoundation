Traffic Sign Classifier & Road Rule Explainer
Agent: When user uploads a traffic sign photo, the agent preprocesses the image, classifies the sign using a model trained on a public road signs dataset, fetches the official meaning from a signs database, and generates a plain-language explanation of the rule along with the penalty for violation.
Frontend: Photo Capture / Upload Page — taking or selecting a photo triggers the agent | Classified Sign Card — agent-identified sign label and confidence percentage | Rule Explanation Panel — agent-fetched official meaning and plain-language explanation | Penalty Information Box — agent-retrieved fine and demerit point data for that sign | Scan History Page — log of all past agent classifications with map pins for where they were taken
