Earthquake Early Warning Notifier
Agent: When user registers their location, the agent streams the real-time earthquake feed, applies a seismic attenuation model to estimate shaking intensity at the user's coordinates for each event, classifies the risk level, sends an immediate push notification for significant events, and logs patterns over time.
Frontend: Location Setup Page — address or GPS; "Activate Alerts" starts the agent | Live Seismic Globe — real-time event markers placed by the agent, sized by magnitude | Notification Panel — recent agent-sent alerts with estimated intensity at the user's location | Historical Frequency Chart — agent-analysed earthquakes by magnitude over the past year | Preparedness Tips Page — agent-personalised advice based on the seismic risk level of the registered location
