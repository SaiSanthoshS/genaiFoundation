Science News Aggregator & Explainer
Agent: When user sets their science domains and reading level, the agent polls space, biology, and physics news sources every morning, classifies articles by domain, fetches relevant images, adapts the language complexity to the chosen reading level, and assembles a personalised daily science digest.
Frontend: Preference Setup Page — domain checkboxes and reading-level slider (beginner to expert); "Activate" triggers daily agent runs | Daily Science Digest Feed — agent-adapted article cards with domain tag, reading time, and featured image | Article Detail Page — agent-simplified text alongside original source link | Saved Articles Bookshelf — user bookmarks; agent flags new articles on the same topic | Trending Topics Word Cloud — agent-generated from the week's digest
