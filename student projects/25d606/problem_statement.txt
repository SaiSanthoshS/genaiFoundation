Smart Study Coach
Agent: When user submits a topic and exam date, the agent fetches concept explanations from encyclopedic sources, finds relevant video lectures, auto-generates a multiple-choice quiz, evaluates the user's answers, and schedules future revision sessions using a spaced-repetition algorithm.
Frontend: Study Setup Form — user enters subject, difficulty level, and exam date, clicks "Build My Plan" | Study Plan Timeline — calendar generated by the agent showing revision sessions and milestones | Quiz Interface — agent-generated MCQ questions, timer, instant feedback after each answer | Progress Dashboard — mastery radial chart and streak counter updated by the agent after each session
