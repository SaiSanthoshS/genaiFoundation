Product Environmental Impact Tracker
Agent: When user scans or searches a product barcode, the agent fetches product data from an open consumer goods database, calculates carbon emissions from production, transport, and packaging, computes an eco-score, and recommends lower-impact alternatives available in the same category.
Frontend: Barcode Scanner / Product Search Page — scan or text input triggers the agent | Eco-Score Card — agent-computed A-to-E grade with total carbon kg displayed prominently | Impact Breakdown Chart — agent-split bar showing production, transport, and packaging emissions | Alternative Products Panel — agent-selected similar items with better eco-scores | Personal Impact Tracker — agent-accumulated running total of carbon saved through better choices
