Personalised News Briefing
Agent: When user saves their interest profile, the agent fetches hundreds of articles every morning, classifies them by topic, scores their sentiment, summarises the top five per category, and assembles a personalised briefing pushed as a notification.
Frontend: Interest Setup Page — user picks topics and sources, clicks "Save & Activate" to start the agent | Daily Briefing Dashboard — tabbed by category, each tab shows agent-generated summaries with sentiment badges | Full Article View — original article with agent-written key-point highlights | Notification Settings — user controls delivery time; agent respects this schedule
