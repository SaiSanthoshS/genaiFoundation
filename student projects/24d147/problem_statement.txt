Freelancer Market Rate Estimator
Agent: When user submits their skills, years of experience, and location, the agent scrapes public freelance job postings, extracts stated hourly rates, normalises them to a common currency, segments by skill combination, and produces a market rate report with median, 25th, and 75th percentile breakdowns.
Frontend: Skills & Profile Input Page — skill tags, experience slider, and country; "Estimate My Rate" triggers the agent | Market Rate Dashboard — agent-computed median and percentile range displayed as a range bar | Rate by Skill Breakdown — agent-generated bar chart per skill combination | Location Comparison Map — agent-calculated rate differences across countries | Negotiation Tips Panel — agent-written advice based on where the user's profile sits in the distribution
