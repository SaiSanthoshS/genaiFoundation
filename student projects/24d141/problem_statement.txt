Personalised Fitness & Diet Planner
Agent: When user submits fitness goals and current stats, the agent generates a weekly workout plan, fetches nutritional data for matching meals, checks the local weather to decide between indoor and outdoor sessions, schedules everything on the user's calendar, and tracks progress weekly.
Frontend: Goal Setup Form — weight, target, activity level; "Build My Plan" triggers the agent | Weekly Plan View — agent-generated workout and meal calendar side by side | Workout Detail Page — agent-selected exercises with sets, reps, and rest periods | Progress Dashboard — agent-tracked weight trend, calorie balance, and streak; user logs results which the agent processes
