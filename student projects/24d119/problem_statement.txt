Ingredient Substitution & Allergy Advisor
Agent: When user pastes a recipe and enters their allergens, the agent scans each ingredient against a food database, flags allergen matches, suggests nutritionally similar safe substitutes for every flagged item, calculates the nutritional difference the substitutions introduce, and outputs the full modified recipe.
Frontend: Recipe & Allergen Input Page — paste recipe text and select allergens; "Check Recipe" triggers the agent | Flagged Ingredients Panel — agent-highlighted ingredients with allergen badges in red | Substitution Cards — agent-suggested replacement alongside original, with nutrition difference table | Modified Recipe View — agent-rewritten full recipe with all substitutions applied | Shopping List — agent-generated list of substitute ingredients to buy
