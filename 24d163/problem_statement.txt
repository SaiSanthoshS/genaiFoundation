Music Mood Playlist Generator
Agent: When user selects a mood, the agent fetches mood-tagged tracks from a public music database, retrieves audio features (tempo, energy, emotional valence), clusters the most fitting tracks, orders them to create a natural emotional arc from start to finish, and builds a 30-track playlist.
Frontend: Mood Input Page — emoji or slider mood selector; "Generate Playlist" triggers the agent | Playlist Preview — agent-assembled track list with waveform visualisation and play buttons | Audio Feature Radar Chart — agent-computed energy, danceability, and valence profile for the whole playlist | Mood Board Page — user saves mood-to-playlist mappings; agent regenerates fresh variants on demand | Export Button — sends the agent-built playlist to a music streaming app
