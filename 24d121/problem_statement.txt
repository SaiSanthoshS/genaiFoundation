1. Collaborative Travel & Itinerary Planner
The Agent: Accepts a list of travelers, their departure cities, budget constraints, and trip duration. It fetches flight schedules, aggregates hotel/Airbnb availability, calculates optimal central destinations (minimizing total travel time/cost), and ranks the top three custom itineraries.
The Frontend:
Input Form: Friend group email list, budget ceiling, travel window, and preferred vibes (e.g., beach, city).
Comparison Grid: A visual matrix showing flight costs, travel durations, and weather overlaps for 3 potential cities.
Itinerary Selector: The top 3 agent-generated trip options with a day-by-day high-level breakdown.
Booking Preview Modal: A confirmation screen showing the aggregated booking links and an agent-drafted "Trip Pitch" email to send to the group.
