Historical Currency & Inflation Analyser
Agent: When user selects currencies and a date range, the agent fetches daily exchange rates, retrieves inflation and purchasing power data from international economic databases, calculates the real value of money over time, and annotates the chart with major economic events that caused visible inflections.
Frontend: Currency & Date Range Selector — dropdowns and date picker; "Analyse" triggers the agent | Exchange Rate Chart — nominal vs inflation-adjusted lines generated by the agent | Purchasing Power Timeline — agent-calculated chart showing real value of a fixed amount across years | Economic Event Annotations — agent-placed markers on the chart; hovering shows a brief agent-written explanation | Report Export Page — agent-compiled PDF with charts and written analysis
