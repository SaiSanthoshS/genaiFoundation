University Admission Profile Evaluator
Agent: When user submits their academic profile, the agent fetches rankings and historical admission statistics for universities matching the target country and field, scores acceptance probability for each based on the user's profile, reviews the uploaded personal statement for clarity and strength, and tracks all application deadlines.
Frontend: Student Profile Input Form — GPA, test scores, and extracurriculars; "Evaluate My Chances" triggers the agent | University Shortlist Page — agent-scored institutions with acceptance probability bars, rank, and estimated fees | Profile vs Admitted Students Chart — agent-overlaid comparison of user's stats against historical admitted cohort | Essay Review Page — agent-assessed readability score and specific improvement suggestions | Deadline Calendar — agent-tracked application deadlines with countdown timers
